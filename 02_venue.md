## Getting there

### By car / parking
Parking is available via **P&R Maastricht Noord**, which is a short walk from Delbrock and has **EV charging points**.

**P&R Maastricht Noord**
Limmelderweg 4, 6222 PB Maastricht

*(Tip: we recommend aiming to arrive a little earlier than you think you need, especially if you’re coming by car.)*

### By train / public transport
**Station Maastricht Noord** is the nearest station and is approximately a **5-minute walk** from Delbrock.
